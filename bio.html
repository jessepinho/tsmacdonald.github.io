Biography

Heralded as “one of the best up-and-coming” players in the country and “a real fiddler”, Tim Macdonald is a regular performer, composer, and teacher of Scottish-Baroque music. A national champion Scottish fiddler, Tim has performed with Rachel Barton Pine, Colyn Fischer, and Alasdair Fraser and Natalie Haas, among others. He made his New York debut in a sold-out performance at the Frick Collection as a guest soloist with Trio Settecento, and has also appeared with the Trio on WQXR classical radio.

Contact Tim to book him for solo performances of any size, Scottish country dances, music workshops, and private lessons (in-person in Chicago or remotely via webcam).
